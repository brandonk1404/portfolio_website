Creation of the Earthly

A series exploring the construction of a treehouse as a symbol of power and ambition. Each piece centers on the Maker figure — a builder whose world-driven creation becomes a metaphor for life shaped by hopes, dreams, and perseverance.

World-building and telling a deeper story are very important to me. The Maker's creation of a world driven by ambition highlights the alternative meaning of creation as a life-giving force.
