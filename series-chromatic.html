Chromatic Fragments

An exhibition exploring the relationship between simple color palettes and negative space — inviting viewers to form meaning from what is absent as much as what is present.

I want viewers to embrace the abstract nature of the work, as well as the varying scale and sense of movement throughout the pieces.
April 27 – May 1, 2026  ·  Mueller Gallery, Caldwell University
